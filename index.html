Phantom Regiment 2023 Judges


This was a mobile-first design with the purpose of informing the staff members of the 2023 Phantom Regiment which judges will be at each show. By clicking on each judge you can see every date, show, and caption assignment that they were assigned to in Summer 2023.


Vanilla JS was used to provide interactiveness within the UI.

Orange County Independent Percussion


OCI is a competitive indoor percussion ensemble based in Orange County, CA. The site was built to sign up & pay for camps/clinics, download an audition packet, promote the group's social media accounts, and post FAQs & info about the ensemble.


A registration form imbedded in the center of the website accepts personal information of prospective memners including a payment portal.
